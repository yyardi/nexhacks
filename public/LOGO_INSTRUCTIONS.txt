ARDEN LOGO SETUP
================

To add your Arden logo:

1. Place your PNG logo file in this directory (/public/)
2. Rename it to: arden-logo.png
3. The logo is already referenced in:
   - /realtime page (header)
   - Landing page (footer)

That's it! The logo will appear automatically.

Recommended dimensions:
- Height: 40-60px (it will auto-scale)
- Format: PNG with transparent background
- Aspect ratio: Keep your original logo proportions
